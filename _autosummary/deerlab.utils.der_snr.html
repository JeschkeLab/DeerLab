deerlab.utils.der_snr#

der_snr(y)[source]#

DER-SNR noise estimation

Estimate the noise level using the DER_SNR method [1].

Parameters:
yarray_like

Noisy dataset.

Returns:
sigmafloat scalar

Noise standard deviation.
